Rami Alturki

Board Chairman

Rami Alturki is the Chairman of Nesma & Partners and President and CEO of Alturki Holding, a diversified Saudi holding company. He also chairs the boards of several subsidiaries and joint ventures, including Velosi Saudi Arabia and Element Saudi Arabia. Alturki began his career in New York with Arthur Andersen after graduating with a degree in Economics from Williams College.

In addition to his corporate leadership, he holds board positions in key national entities such as the Saudi Authority for Industrial Cities and Technology Zones (MODON), the King Fahad Causeway Authority, and was previously a member of the Saudi Standards, Metrology and Quality Organization (SASO). He is also deeply involved in education, youth development, and community service, serving on the advisory board of Dhahran Ahliyya Schools and the boards of Endeavor Saudi Arabia, Shabab Mujtamie, Al Qadsiah Club, the Disabled Association Eastern Province, Ta’allum Association, and other non-profit organizations across Saudi Arabia.

Faisal Alturki

Board Member

In addition to serving on the board at Nesma & Partners, Faisal Alturki is the President of Nesma Holding Company (one of Nesma & Partners’ four shareholders), which operates across multiple sectors throughout Dubai, Egypt, and Turkey. 

Alturki has personally been involved in several of Nesma’s CSR initiatives, most notably including creating public parks in Jeddah to strengthen social advancement. 

Passionate about driving forward innovation & infrastructure in the region, Alturki oversaw Nesma Holding’s entry into several industries, including the airline & travel industry, renewable energy, public transportation, and fast food.

A serial entrepreneur, Alturki founded Nesma Airlines and served as Chairman of the Board for two other Nesma operations (Nesma Concrete Solutions and Nesma Water & Energy Company). He also maintains board seats with five additional companies throughout the region and serves as the President of two other companies, Nesma Trading, and Mawaddah International Group Co.

Noura Alturki

Board Member

Noura Alturki is Vice President of Nesma, a family business with over 40 years of robust experience across multiple sectors in Saudi Arabia, with a presence in the UAE, Egypt, and Turkey.

A dynamic and seasoned leader, she is involved in corporate governance as a member of numerous subsidiary boards and board committees. She has participated in the boards of public organizations to promote transparency and accountability, serving as Vice President of the Saudi Shooting Federation from 2018 to 2021. 

She is devoted to inspiring workplaces that embody excellence, innovation, diversity, respect, and impact.

Passionate about diversity & inclusion in the workplace, she spearheaded Namat, a social enterprise Nesma established that integrates women outside urban centers into the national economy and redefines the potential of ‘Made in KSA.’ She has also participated in the International Labor Conference, where 2012, she led the discussion on youth employment, and in previous years joined the drafting committees for United Nations recommendations and standards regarding gender equality and global rights for domestic workers.

Alturki holds a BA in Environmental Studies from Brown University and a Master of Science in Nature, Society, and Environmental Policy from the University of Oxford. She has a Diploma in Company Direction from the UK Institute of Directors.

Outside of work, she loves to travel abroad and spend time with her children and family.

Dr. Zeyad Al-Hekail

Board Member

Dr. Zeyad O. Al-Hekail is a seasoned executive and board leader with over three decades of experience across the private, public, and nonprofit sectors in Saudi Arabia. He brings a strategic vision rooted in governance, investment leadership, and organizational transformation, making him a valuable addition to the Board of Directors of Nesma & Partners. Dr. Al-Hekail currently serves as Chairman or Board Member of several leading entities, including Advanced Communications and Electronics Systems Company (ACES), SEDCO Holding, Majd Investment Company, Makkah Development and Construction Company, and Shaheen AI. He was also a Board Member and Compliance Committee Chair at Albilad Bank and a founding investor in Tadweer Recycling Company. His leadership spans diverse sectors such as finance, technology, real estate, aquaculture, and education. Notably, he served as CEO of Suliman Alrajhi Holding Company and Advanced Educational Company and held senior academic roles at King Saud University, including Deputy Rector for Educational and Academic Affairs. In the nonprofit domain, Dr. Al-Hekail has contributed significantly to endowments and social development. He currently sits on the Board of Trustees of the King Abdullah Endowment in Madinah and Princess Nourah University. He previously chaired the Board of Trustees of the Sakan Foundation and participated in various strategic committees supporting national education, healthcare, and economic development. Dr. Al-Hekail holds a Ph.D. and M.S. in Electrical Engineering from Ohio State University and a B.S. in Electrical Engineering from King Saud University. His multidisciplinary expertise, strategic governance acumen, and commitment to sustainable development align with Nesma & Partners' mission of building excellence and contributing to Saudi Arabia's Vision 2030.

Ahmed AlGhamdi

Board Member

Ahmad AlGhamdi joined the Board of Directors at Nesma & Partners in March 2023. 

In addition to his work with Nesma & Partners, he oversees MENA Investments at the Public Investment Funds (PIF). He holds Board seats at the Southern Province Cement Company & JASARA Program Management Company. 

Before his role at the PIF, AlGhamdi spent a decade building a career with Saudi Aramco, where he helped to design and implement an ambitious corporate transformation. During his tenure, he helped the company develop a competitive local energy sector, invest in new businesses to enrich its income streams and overhaul many policies and procedures in pursuit of better efficiency and agility in an increasingly competitive market. 

AlGhamdi holds a Bachelor's degree in Chemical Engineering from McGill University in Canada and a Master’s in Business Administration from Harvard University in the United States.

Nasser AlHuqbani

Board Member

Nasser Al Huqbani is a multifaceted and result-oriented business leader with more than two decades of experience in the health sector. He has been the CEO of the Health Holding Company since January 2023 and a member of the Executive Committee of the National Health Committee of the Council of Saudi Chambers since 2020. He is also a member of the Health Insurance Strategic National Committee since 2020, the Saudi-Russian Joint Economic Committee since 2019, the Board of Trustees of the Saudi Commission for Health Specialties since 2017, the Board of Directors of the Tawuniya Insurance Company since 2023, and the Board of Directors of the Nesma and Partners Company since 2023.

Al Huqbani has achieved numerous accomplishments throughout his career. He holds a Bachelor's degree in Administrative Sciences and Public Administration from King Saud University in Riyadh in 1992 and a Master's degree in Office Management from the Institute of Public Administration in Riyadh in 1994. He has also won numerous awards, including the Guinness World Record for the largest Tele-ICU Command Center in the world (2019), the Leadership Award 2019 - "Best CEO in Health Care," the 11th Leading CEO Excellence Award 2014, and the CEO Middle East KSA Awards 2009.

Carlos F. Aguilar

Independent Board Member

Dr. Carlos F. Aguilar is an independent Board Member at Nesma & Partners.

Dr. Carlos F. Aguilar is a seasoned executive with over 30 years of experience in project finance, engineering, and large-scale infrastructure development, investment, and execution. Appointed as an Independent Board Member by the Public Investment Fund (PIF), he brings strategic leadership and global expertise in power, oil & gas, transport, large-scale multiuse facilities, and industrial projects.

With degrees in Mechanical Engineering from Duke University and Technological Economics from the University of Stirling, Carlos supports Nesma & Partners’ commitment to innovation and excellence in construction and infrastructure.

Bushra Abu Salem

Board Secretary and Governance & Compliance Affairs Director

Bushra Abu Salem is Governance & Compliance Affairs Director, heading the Governance and Compliance Department at Nesma & Partners, reporting to the Board of Directors. She has also been the secretary of the Board of Directors and most of its Committees at Nesma & Partners from 2015 to 2022, ensuring compliance at the corporate level.

Her competence and knowledge stem from over 23 years of cross-functional experience in the legal field, corporate governance, and corporate affairs.

Throughout her tenure at Nesma & Partners, Abu Salem has studied, assessed, and compiled reports addressing operating governance at the company. She has grown her expertise in corporate governance, intellectual property, information technology, FIDIC, commercial contracts, contracting business, oil and gas, and more, to assume high-profile positions, namely serving as the Corporate Affairs Executive Manager covering the scope of Chief of Staff with the co-founder and late President & CEO of Nesma & Partners, Imad Gholmie.  

In 2015, Bushra established the Management Committee with the late President & CEO. In 2016, she worked on establishing the Corporate Governance framework in the company, which was approved by the current Shareholder, formulating the Terms of Reference for the Board and its committees. Today, she is heading the Governance & Compliance Department, where she also worked on establishing its roles and responsibilities, setting defined KPIS for the team.

 As a legal advisor and Board Secretary, Abu Salem’s role focuses on protecting the interests of the company, its shareholders, and its employees. Not only does she provide essential support to the Chairman, President & CEO, and other directors, but she also is the primary contact for all shareholders as her role focuses on maintaining and fostering good communication among the Board Directors, providing legal advice on critical issues.

Her robust career with Nesma & Partners features engagement with high-end company projects.

Before joining Nesma & Partners, Abu Salem worked in public and international organizations such as ESCWA, the International Chamber of Commerce, and the World Trade Organization, to name a few.

Abu Salem is an associate with the Chartered Institute of Arbitrators in the UK and GCC BDI Certified Board Secretary. She is also a certified Ethics Officer and a Certified Compliance Professional.

Abu Salem has a bachelor’s degree in law from the Lebanese University and a master’s in Business Law from La Sagesse University with a specialty in Islamic Banking Regulations. She is in the process of completing her Master of Arts in Advanced Studies in International Affairs at the Lebanese American University (LAU).

 

Samer Abdul Samad

President & Chief Executive Officer

Samer Abdul Samad is the President & Chief Executive Officer (CEO) of Nesma & Partners.

Samer joined the Company in 1994 as a site engineer and progressed steadily until he was promoted to Vice President of Public Works in 2016. In 2020, he was appointed as the Executive Vice President and Chief Operating Officer before his current appointment as the President and CEO in 2021.

During his previous roles, Samer was an indispensable member of the Operations Department overseeing all construction and infrastructure projects at Nesma & Partners.

Throughout his tenure as the President and CEO, Samer has worked to solidify the company’s positioning as a leading contracting company in the Kingdom. In early 2022, Samer shared his strategy and vision for Nesma & Partners within the organization to build on the company’s 40 years of legacy as a proud Saudi company, operating nationwide, progressively, regionally, and internationally.

Under his leadership, Nesma & Partners has won many mega projects like the Red Sea Airport and Riyadh Avenues and successfully delivered many projects for government and private clients in Riyadh, NEOM, al-Ula, and Eastern Province.

Samer has a Master of Science in Civil Engineering from New York University, Tandon School of Engineering.

Haytham Kalash

He has honed his skills and expertise through roles at esteemed international entities such as ABB and SNC Lavalin, further enriching his vast knowledge base.

Kent

Kent is a privately-owned international integrated energy services partner acquired by Nesma & Partners. Founded in 1919 as a small family company in Ireland, Kent is now a 13,000-strong client-centric team and $1.4 billion-dollar revenue global business.  It delivers sustainable and innovative engineering services and project delivery solutions for the oil and gas, industrial, renewables, and low-carbon industries. It has a roster of blue-chip clients, including international energy companies, national oil companies, renewable energy companies, and global petrochemical companies.

Nesma & Partners – Sicim

Nesma & Partners, the leading contracting company in Saudi Arabia, has announced a landmark joint venture with Sicim, a renowned Italian firm. This strategic partnership, aimed at two Master Gas projects involving pipeline engineering, procurement, and construction for nearly 500 kilometers of 56” size across the mountainous western region of the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia between Taif and South Jeddah, is a significant step towards enhancing the energy sector's capabilities in the Kingdom, marking a pivotal moment in the region's pipeline development.

NASJV

The Joint Venture of NASJV between Nesma & Partners, Almabani General Contractors, and Advanced Telecom Systems for Contracting Company was formed in January 2020 to execute two contracts for the construction, procurement, and development of King Faisal Air Academy (“KFAA”) and King Salman Air Base (“KSAB”) in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia for Ministry of Defense.

NAPJV

The Joint Venture of NAPJV between Nesma & Partners-Almabani General Contractors was formed in January 2020 to execute a single contract for the construction, procurement, and development of airside works for Red Sea Airport in connection with Red Sea Development Company.

Nesma & Partners – MAN Enterprise JV

Nesma & Partners announced a new joint venture with MAN Enterprise, a globally recognized construction and project management firm. This strategic partnership aims to drive the development of the Qurain Cultural District, a key component of the Diriyah project that seamlessly blends cultural heritage with modern urban living.

Al Yusr AYTB

AYTB is a leading Saudi, multi-disciplinary industrial contracting company providing a range of services and solutions to oil & gas, petrochemical, power generation and other major industrial sectors of Saudi Arabia. Boasting over 40 years of experience in the fields of plant project services, manufacturing, industrial services, operations and maintenance, and housing and catering, AYTB has more than 6,000 dedicated employees willing to serve, seeking to fulfill their stakeholders’ aspirations and clients’ satisfaction.

Imad Ventures

Imad Ventures is the corporate venture capital arm of Nesma & Partners, established at the end of 2024 to support the development of a more modern construction industry. Guided by the slogan "For a better built environment," the firm acts as a strategic "pillar" (Imad) for the sector by investing in early-stage Pre-Seed, Seed, and Series A startups within the Built World. Since beginning its investment activity in 2025, Imad Ventures has focused on practical and innovative technologies that help make building safer, faster, and more sustainable at scale. By connecting promising construction tech ventures with the deep industry experience of Nesma & Partners, Imad Ventures helps provide the groundwork to improve how energy, infrastructure, and building projects are delivered today.

About us

Together,
We Build Excellence

Nesma & Partners group is the leading provider of integrated project solutions in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia. With a strong legacy in contracting, Nesma & Partners has grown its footprint both locally through its contracting and industrial services and globally through its wholly owned subsidiary, Kent, a renowned leader in engineering and project management services.

Our Story

What began in 1981 as a contracting firm to meet the growing demands of an emerging economy in the rapidly developing Kingdom of Saudi Arabia, has evolved today into a diversified group delivering end-to-end, reliable, and innovative services across the energy, infrastructure, and building sectors, spanning consulting, engineering, construction, commissioning, operations, and maintenance.

1981

Nesma & Al Fadl

Nesma & Partners was established in 1981 as “Nesma & Al Fadl”

1995

Nesma-Emcor

Partnered up with Nesma-Emcor to power electro-mechanical construction works.

1997

Nesma-AGAP

Partnered up with Nesma-AGAP to break into the oil & gas contracting sector.

2004

The Merger

The union of our strengths led to the foundation of Nesma & Partners.

2010

1,000,000,000+ Revenues

By 2010, our revenues exceeded SAR 1 billion.

2022

Public Investment Fund (PIF)

In November 2022, the Public Investment Fund (PIF) was welcomed as a partner in Nesma & Partners.

2024

Kent's Acquisition

In January 2024, Nesma & Partners acquired Kent, a renowned global leader in engineering and project management within the energy sector.

2025

Sudlows Acquisition

In February 2025, Kent acquired Sudlows Consulting, a leading design and engineering firm specializing in data centers.

2025

AYTB Acquisition

In July 2025, Nesma & Partners acquired Al Yusr Industrial Contracting Company (AYTB), a prominent provider of industrial services in the Kingdom.

Our Mission

To deliver projects in partnership with our clients: Safer, with zero loss time injuries. Better, with zero quality rejections. And faster, by always meeting deadlines.

Vision

To be the premier integrated project solutions provider across the energy, infrastructure, and buildings sectors, entrusted to transform ambitious visions into reality in Saudi Arabia and beyond.

Our Values

Excellence

We strive for excellence in everything we do. We set high standards for ourselves and continuously seek opportunities for improvement, delivering the highest quality in our contracting and service solutions.

Respect

We treat everyone with respect and appreciation. We conduct our business with unwavering integrity, honesty and transparency, adhering to the highest ethical standards and treating our clients, employees and stakeholders fairly and respectfully.

Innovation

Innovation has been our main pillar throughout our journey. We embrace innovation as a driving force for success. We encourage creativity, exploration of new ideas, and the adoption of cutting-edge technologies and methodologies. We continuously adapt to changes in the industry and strive to be at the forefront of innovation.

Diversity & Collaboration

We embrace diversity in all forms, and we believe that by working together and leveraging our diverse skills and expertise, we can achieve greater results and deliver innovative solutions to our clients.

Impact
